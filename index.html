    About

    My hobby that I do in my free time is painting. Painting enhances my creativity, and allows me to capture my imagination, thoughts in an exquisite and realistic portrait. In addition to improving innovativity, painting is also remarked as a great engaging stress-buster activity due to its dominance in improving mental health. Painting gives us an opportunity to explore different color combinations and helps us reflect on the masterpiece. The process of giving life, journey to a canvas is enjoyed by people of all ages, from children to adults. Overall, painting is prominent in helping us concentrate, broadens our thinking skills, and attracts positivity to ourselves.
